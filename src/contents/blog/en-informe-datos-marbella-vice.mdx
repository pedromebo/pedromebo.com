> ## Introduction

Marbella Vice is a GTA RP server founded by *Ibai Llanos* and *Jacky* (Coolifegame) aimed at content creators. The server was developed by the *MoleCorp* team, bringing all their experience and customization capabilities. The creators were directly invited through a passport system or via applications.

The entry process for creators was not the same for everyone, as they joined at different dates.

This project measures the data from the moment a creator's entry is publicly announced by their community or themselves. This means that some creators might not be included in the lists, and thus their content has not been monitored.

Unlike most Twitch analytics websites, this project had a refresh rate (the frequency at which data is collected) of 1 minute, making it more precise. However, the data is calculated independently and *is not provided directly by Twitch*, so while every effort is made to ensure accuracy, the data may not be exact.

In this report, you will find both general server information and specific creator data.

> ## General Data

### Total Viewers
An estimated total of **26,949,099 non-unique viewers**

### Total Hours Watched
A total of:
#### **113,531,617** **hours** have been watched.
That is **4,730,484 days**, 

equivalent to **12,960 years**.

### Total Hours Created
From **6,921 live streams**, a total of:
#### **29,557** **hours** have been created.
That is **1,231 days**,

equivalent to **3.37 years**.

So if you plan to watch all of Marbella Vice and dedicate 8 hours a day, you would finish **in 10 years and 43 days.**

> ## Future

We already know that Marbella Vice has been incredible and that many creators have significantly increased their reach. Now, many of them face a harsh reality: the challenge of maintaining all those new followers, whether in GTA V or other types of content.

In the future, we will analyze how these creators have evolved, if their growth rate continues, and if they are able to maintain their numbers. I encourage you to follow me on Twitter to stay updated and be the first to know about new content.

If you've read this far, thank you so much! It has been a pleasure to share my passion for data with so many people, meet fantastic streamers, and receive support from different communities.

Once again, thank you.
